# Music Download Project Ideas

## Beginner

- **Music file organizer** — Scan a folder, fix naming conventions, deduplicate files, sort into Artist/Album structure
- **Metadata tagger** — Auto-fetch and apply album art, lyrics, genre, and track info from APIs like MusicBrainz
- **Download manager** — Handle retries for failed downloads, verify file integrity, track progress

## Intermediate

- **Audio quality validator** — Detect fake lossless files (e.g., an MP3 upconverted to FLAC) using spectral analysis
- **Music library dashboard** — Show storage usage, format breakdown, missing metadata, duplicates, and quality stats
- **Format converter** — Batch convert between formats (FLAC, MP3, OGG, AAC) with preset quality profiles
- **Lyrics fetcher** — CLI or GUI tool that matches your library against lyrics databases and embeds them in files
- **Smart sync tool** — Keep a music library in sync across devices/folders with conflict resolution

## Advanced

- **Self-hosted music server** — Stream your downloaded library to any device (like a personal Spotify)
- **P2P file sharing app** — Decentralized music sharing with search, resumable transfers, and integrity checks
- **Music discovery engine** — Analyze your library's audio features and recommend similar music from public catalogs
- **DRM-free store aggregator** — Search across Bandcamp, Amazon, etc. to find the best price/format for a given album

## Niche/Fun

- **Playlist portability tool** — Convert playlists between Spotify/YouTube/Apple Music and local file playlists
- **Album completeness checker** — Scan your library against a database and flag missing tracks from albums you own
- **Malware scanner for media files** — Validate that audio files are actually audio and not disguised executables

## DJ Software Project Ideas

### Performance Tools
- **Key/BPM analyzer** — Scan a music library and tag tracks with key and BPM, flag compatible pairs
- **Set planner** — Input your tracks, get suggested orderings based on key, BPM, and energy flow
- **Cue point generator** — Analyze track structure (intro, drop, outro) and auto-suggest cue points
- **Live setlist tracker** — Record what you played, when, and export it for social media or royalty reporting

### Library Management
- **Music library health checker** — Find duplicates, low-bitrate files, missing tags, corrupt files
- **Crate builder** — Auto-generate crates/playlists by genre, mood, energy, key, or BPM range
- **Cross-platform library converter** — Sync playlists/cues between Rekordbox, Serato, and Traktor

### Crowd/Gig Tools
- **Request manager** — Web app where the crowd submits requests, you approve/reject from your phone
- **Crowd energy tracker** — Use mic input or phone accelerometer data to gauge dance floor energy over time
- **Gig CRM** — Track venues, promoters, pay rates, past setlists, and notes per gig

### Practice & Learning
- **Beatmatch trainer** — App that plays two tracks and scores your manual beatmatching accuracy
- **Transition logger** — Record practice sessions, tag transitions as clean or rough, review later
- **Harmonic mixing quiz** — Drill key compatibility using the Camelot wheel

### Business Side
- **Invoice/contract generator** — Templates for DJ gigs with terms, rates, and gear requirements
- **Promo kit builder** — Generate EPKs from your mixes, bio, and photos
